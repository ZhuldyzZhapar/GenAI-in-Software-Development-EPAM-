# Vocabulary (Key Terms)

Below is a glossary of key terms used in this research, explained in simple language. Generative AI (GenAI): AI that creates new content â€” text, audio, code, video, or design. Artificial Neural Networks (ANN): Computer models inspired by the human brain. Natural Neural Networks (NNN): Biological neural networks in the human brain. Attention Mechanism: AI technique that focuses on important parts of data for better results. State-of-the-art (SOTA): The most advanced level of technology in a given field. Vibe Coding: Intuitive coding style based on flow and experience rather than strict rules. Natural Language Communication: Interaction with computers using everyday human language. Web AI-chat: Browser-based AI chat (e.g., ChatGPT, DeepSeek). Plugin: Extension that adds new functionality to software (e.g., Figma, VS Code). Companion: AI assistant that supports users with tasks, notes, and learning. AI-native Tools: Applications built around AI from the start (e.g., Notion AI, Runway). Agentic Platforms: Systems where AI acts autonomously, planning and executing tasks.
